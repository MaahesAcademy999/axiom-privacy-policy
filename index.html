Privacy Policy for Axiom

Effective date: July 28, 2026

Axiom ("the App") is a habit-tracking game. This policy explains what data the App handles, and it is short on purpose: by default, Axiom collects nothing at all.

Local-only by default

Out of the box, Axiom stores everything — your quests, stats, achievements, reading history, and settings — only on your own device. No account is required, no data is transmitted anywhere, and the App works fully offline, forever, unless you choose otherwise.

Optional cloud account

Axiom offers an opt-in "Cloud Sync" feature so you can keep the same save across multiple devices. It is off by default. If you choose to create a cloud account, we collect:

  • Email address and password — used only to authenticate you. Passwords are hashed and managed by our backend provider, Supabase (see below); we never see or store your password in plain text.
  • A display name you choose yourself.
  • A randomly generated friend code — not tied to your real identity.
  • A compact snapshot of your game progress — character stats and levels, total XP, streak count, IDs of completed quests and unlocked achievements, your in-game character name, entitlement status, equipment/cosmetic unlocks, skill tree progress, your fitness-level setting, and an estimated "gold saved" figure.

What we never collect, sync, or have any way to see

  • Birthdate and birth time — even if you voluntarily enter them in the optional Profile section for the cosmetic zodiac-sign display, they never leave your device. This field structurally never syncs to our servers.
  • Body measurements (weight, height, or similar) — Axiom has no such feature.
  • Your detailed activity log, custom quest text, reading session details, or daily quest history — these stay local to your device only.
  • Location data.
  • Advertising identifiers, analytics, or any behavioral tracking.
  • Contacts, photos, or any device data beyond what's listed above.

Third-party service provider

Axiom's cloud features are built on Supabase, which provides authentication and database hosting on our behalf. Access to your data is restricted at the database level so that only you can ever read or write your own account's data — not other users, and not us, through the app itself.

Data retention and deletion

You can disconnect your cloud account at any time from within the App — this immediately stops any further syncing but does not by itself delete your existing data from our servers.

To permanently delete your cloud account and all associated data, email Maahesacademy999@gmail.com with the email address you signed up with, and we will delete it within a reasonable time. Deleting your cloud account never affects your local, on-device save — that data stays on your device regardless.

Before launch: Google Play requires either an in-app deletion flow or a reachable web-based deletion request path for any app with account creation — see the checklist notes for what still needs to be built here.

Children's privacy

Axiom is designed to be appropriate for a general audience, including teens. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a child under 13 has created a cloud account without appropriate consent, contact us at the email above and we will delete the associated data.

No ads, no selling of data

Axiom displays no advertisements. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes, under any circumstances.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the App evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated effective date.
